WEB 3 - OUR VISION

Web 3 social networks are a new type of social network that use blockchain technology to decentralize the platform and give users more control over their data. These networks are powered by smart contracts, which allow for greater transparency and trust between users. They also provide better privacy and security than traditional social networks, as well as faster transaction speeds. Additionally, they have built-in incentives for users to participate in the network, such as rewards for content creation or curation.

Web 3 social networks are still in their early stages of development but have the potential to revolutionize how people interact online.
